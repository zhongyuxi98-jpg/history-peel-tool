# GeekGirl Visual PEEL AI Prompts (v1.0)

## 🎯 Prompt 1: Point Generator (解题引导)
**使用场景**：学生拿到题目，不知道怎么下笔，需要 AI 辅助定调。

> "You are a senior A-Level History coach. I am using a PEEL sticker system to plan my essay.
>
> **Task:** Based on the question below, generate 3 distinct 'Thesis Points' (L0). 
> **Requirement:** Each point must start with a logical direction (e.g., Cause, Extent, or Turning Point).
>
> **Question:** [在此输入题目]
>
> **Output Format:**
> 1. [Direction] + [Core Concept]
> 2. [Direction] + [Core Concept]
> 3. [Direction] + [Core Concept]"

---

## 🔍 Prompt 2: PEEL Structure Checker (结构校对)
**使用场景**：学生摆好了贴纸，需要 AI 检查逻辑是否通顺。

> "Check my PEEL structure for logic and depth.
>
> **My Layout:**
> - **Point (Meta + Content):** [在此输入贴纸内容]
> - **Evidence (L1/L2/L3):** [在此输入贴纸内容]
> - **Mechanism (Explain):** [在此输入连接逻辑]
> - **Link:** [在此输入总结句]
>
> **Feedback Request:** 
> 1. Does the Evidence support the Point?
> 2. Is the Mechanism explained clearly?
> 3. Give me a score (1-5) for 'Analytical Depth' and 1 tip to improve."

---

## 📚 Prompt 3: Knowledge Deep-Dive (知识补完)
**使用场景**：学生不理解某个贴纸背后的深层含义。

> "Explain this historical concept to me like I am an A-Level student. 
> Focus on its **impact** and **why it matters** in the context of the essay.
>
> **Concept:** [在此输入贴纸名称, e.g., NAACP]"
